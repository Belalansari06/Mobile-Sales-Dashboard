# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview

This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze mobile phone sales across multiple Indian cities. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, payment preferences, and product trends, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of sales data every day. Without a centralized dashboard, it becomes difficult to monitor sales performance, identify high-performing products, understand customer behavior, and track business trends.

---

## 🚀 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Track product-wise sales
- Analyze city-wise sales distribution
- Understand customer ratings
- Evaluate payment method preferences
- Monitor monthly sales trends

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- 💰 Total Sales
- 📦 Total Quantity Sold
- 💳 Total Transactions
- 💵 Average Selling Price

---

## ✨ Dashboard Features

- Dynamic filters (Month, Brand, Model, Payment Method)
- KPI Cards
- City-wise Sales Map
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Customer Rating Analysis
- Payment Method Distribution
- Brand Performance Analysis
- Daily Sales Trend

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Compare sales performance across brands and mobile models.
- Identify cities generating the highest revenue.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Monitor monthly sales trends.
- Evaluate customer satisfaction using ratings.

## 📈 Business Value

This dashboard helps businesses to:

- Improve inventory planning
